Simulator

Simulator is used as an all encompassing term, which includes the skillsets listed below.

  • Modeling and Simulation

    • Chanakya is proficient in modeling an simulation related to mining and metals. The services include:

  • Development and utilization of simulation tools to achieve operational excellence. The activites supported are planning, debottlenecking, evaluating investment options, cost and risk analysis. These tools are transferrable across various process industries to achieve goals, study financial and insurance trade cycles. The technologies utilized for the purpose are chemical/metallurgical first principles, discrete event simulation, monte-carlo. The tools which could be utilized for the purpose are commerical softwares like METSIM, FLEXSIM, complex spreadsheet models, and customized numerical solutions using programming languages such as VBA and Python. However, the license cost for commerical softwares for the time of use has to be borne by the clients
